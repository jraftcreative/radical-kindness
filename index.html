Kevin Wee
About Kevin

A Simple Human Being Who Believes Life's Purpose Is To Love

Kevin Wee is a speaker, author, edupreneur and creator who overcame severe mental health challenges including depression, mania, and suicidal ideation. Today, he channels that experience into holding space for others who are struggling — proving that your darkest chapters can become your greatest source of light.

With over 10 years of training and speaking experience, Kevin has built multiple ventures generating 7 figures in annual revenue collectively, while simultaneously running two social enterprises dedicated to uplifting communities.

"It is the blend of self-efficacy and surrender that makes a robust resiliency. Thesis and antithesis leads to synthesis — an enduring balance between the two."
The Movement

Radical Kindness

What started as a personal transformation became a movement. Radical Kindness is a media platform that has amassed over 100K followers, generated millions in reach, and raised over $100K — all given directly to those in need within 8 months of inception.

From Suicide Notes to Kindness

Kevin distributed $10,000 in envelopes to strangers. Those envelopes were originally intended as suicide notes — transformed into vessels of hope and radical generosity.

$50K in One Week

When a community member needed cancer treatment, the Radical Kindness community rallied to raise $50,000 in just seven days.

A Growing Movement

From content that goes viral to conversations that change lives, Radical Kindness is redefining what it means to show up for one another.
